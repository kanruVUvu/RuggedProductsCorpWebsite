Management

Peter Hartmut Voelkner (CEO)

Peter Hartmut Voelkner (CEO) founded RUGGED PRODUCTS CORP (RPC) in September 2020 to focus on off-grid and mobile renewable energy solutions in North America. The company leverages the manufacturing, design, and engineering capabilities of RUGGED SOLAR PRODUCTS (India), providing industrial grade, super-durable, and high-efficiency photovoltaic products and systems exclusively to RPC.  Peter brings over 30 years of experience in specialized off-grid solar power systems, and he has over a decade of experience in distributed power and storage technologies.  Furthermore, he spent 20 years working in finance, based in New York City.  Over his career Peter performed in-depth due-diligence on hundreds of companies and directed significant long-term investments in more than 50 public companies. He is experienced in operating manufacturing and service businesses, establishing and growing start-ups, and performing M&A related due-diligence. His extensive network within the financial community globally supports the strategic plans for the companies he runs. Peter received a MBA from Columbia Business School at Columbia University, NY. He graduated with a BA in Economics and Chinese Studies from Bennington College, VT.

Vincent Guacci (CFO)

Vincent Guacci (CFO) has over 20 years experience as a Chief Financial Officer in the financial services industry. He has managed all of financial, legal and technology requirements at several major investment firms, including Sansar Capital Management, a leading international hedge fund with peak assets of $3.5 billion. Vince is highly experienced in accounting, audit, corporate finance and private equity, risk management, compliance and investor relations. He has launched multiple startups, where he implemented business processes and infrastructure from the ground up, and maintains an extensive network of relationships with financial firms, seed investors, and investment bankers. Vince holds a M.S. in Technology Management specializing in Financial Engineering from Stevens Institute of Technology as well as a B.S. in Finance from Montclair State University.
