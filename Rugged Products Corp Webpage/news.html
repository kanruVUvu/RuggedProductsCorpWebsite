News


9 September 2020

RUGGED PRODUCTS CORP (RPC) is established as a Delaware C-Corp with initial offices in Austin, TX USA. The company is committed to providing the best and most durable off-grid and mobile power solutions using innovative photovoltaic and power storage technologies. RPC targets industrial OEMs, industrial after-market distributors, as well as commercial and consumer users with its proprietary RUGGEDFLEX™ and RUGGEDSOLAR™ lines of photovoltaic modules, MADE STRONG by the Monolithic Casting process only available from RUGGED.

11 November 2020

The RPC Amazon Store is launched, offering three RUGGEDFLEX™ modules for consumers in North America. The RUGGEDFLEX™ solar module is MADE STRONG with no lamination or glass parts. The Monolithic Casting manufacturing process creates a totally waterproof panel rated at IPX67. The modules are frameless and ultra-thin (less than ¼” thicknes at the panel and less than ¾” thickness including the fully waterproofed terminal covers), and semi-flexible for installation on a rigid surface with a bend of up to 30°. Unlike other flexible solar panels available in the market, RUGGEDFLEX™ modules will not suffer from water intrusion into the PV cells or terminal connections giving hassle-free performance with a five-years warranty.
